Best Heroes in Kingshot (Tier List) – Gen 3 + New Epics

3Investing in your heroes in Kingshot is crucial but there are some pitfalls that you have to know about or you can end up slowing your progress down by a lot – with every generation your server grows, new heroes will be added and investing in Exclusive Gear of a hero that becomes less useful might be a huge mistake.

For this reason, I have created a tier list below that helps you understand what heroes you’re aiming for to max out and which ones you will probably replace sooner or later in Kingshot.

The Best Heroes in Kingshot

Before we get into the list, please undertand some fundamental things about heroes and servers in Kingshot. The age of your server determines what heroes are available to you, so you will see the heroes in the tier list below ordered by generations.

A rough overview of the generations is:

  • 1st generation server are younger than 40 days
  • NEW! 2nd generation server are older than 40 days rolling out on the earliest servers
  • SOON 3rd generation server are older than 120 days
  • SOON 4th generation server are older than 200 days
  • …

This means, new heroes become available to your server after about 5-6 Lucky Wheels (every 80 days) and that means that the newer heroes normally will change the roaster of heroes. To read the tier list below correctly, you have to check what heroes are available on your server and what generation your server is and then look at the section to see the correct ranking of the heroes. Amazing heroes on 1st generation servers in Kingshot aren’t that great on 5th generation servers anymore and so on…

But that also means that it is hard to say “this is the best hero” or “build this hero” since newer heroes are typically stronger but you can’t just wait it out until they are available. For this reason our list below is sorted by the Generation a hero is released in but also showing how a hero performs in a later generation. So if you build a Generation 2 hero to see how many generations that hero will actually be viable in Kingshot.
