Swordland Showdown Strategy Guide

Event Overview

Preparation

  • For leader: Only register who are actually going to show up. Higher show up rate, garantee the win.
  • For leader: use labels and markers to guide players, attach or defense
  • Advanced: Organize alliance members into Attack (strong players), Support (strongest & whale), and Defense teams.
  • Activate all buffs, gear, and pet skill (Moose -> Horror Stare) before battle begins.
  • Use Discord or voice chat to coordinate roles and strategy.
  • Save healing speedups every day.
  • If your troops are wiped, you can retreat and come back after 12 minutes to get them all back.

Prerequisites

  • Only the top 20 alliances can participate.
  • Each alliance registers two Legions (30 fighters + 10 substitutes each).
  • Recall all troops before joining, no marching, healing, or reinforcing.

Goal

  • Capture and hold buildings to earn Relic Points.
  • Win by having the highest Relic Point total at the end of the match.
  • If you are losing and outnumbered, farm points and attack buildings to gain points for higher rewards

Battle Phases

  • Phase 1: Capture basic buildings (Sanctums, Abbeys, Belltower, Royal Stables).
  • Phase 2: At the 15-minute mark, Stronger buildings open (Swordshrine, Hall of Reformation, Mercenary Camp).
  • Phase 3: Undercellers appear randomlyâ€”loot for bonus points.
